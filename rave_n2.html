The discovery of other music genres started in the second issue of Raveolution, message.

The music genre that gave the name for the second issue is hip hop / spoken word. In this genre, some artists use the power of lyrics to expose social and political issues. Kojey Radical and Kate Tempest give us a glimpse of their process.

And then passing through Brazil and Lebanon, we see how techno is still empowering people.
