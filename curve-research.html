CURVE is a conceptual and fictional micronation. CURVE’s goal is to question the present and future standarts of living in our planet in different scenarios and balance them.

Right now, to speculate about the future, what we need to do is to focus in a certain scenario and try to prevent it. Take COVID as an example and focus on flattening the curve to minimize the damage that might cause. But here comes the tricky part: by focusing in one curve, we cannot forget about all the others around, because they will also be influenced as well. It’s all connected.

Focus in the balance. A conscious approach to flattening the curve in all the aspects, so we can have a balanced future, resulting in an harmonious society with different values.
